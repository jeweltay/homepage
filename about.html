My Journey

For the past six years, I've dedicated my career to education and digital initiatives, leading projects that bridge gaps and create meaningful impact across diverse teams and communities.

As a PMP-certified project manager, I've honed my skills in turning complex visions into executable plans, always with a focus on human-centered design and cross-cultural collaboration. My multilingual abilities have been instrumental in connecting global teams and ensuring clear communication across borders.

My journey into technology represents a natural evolution of this path. I've always been fascinated by how technology can transform industries and improve lives, and I see coding as a new language that enables me to build solutions rather than just manage them.

Through Harvard's CS50 course, I'm building the technical foundation to complement my project management expertise. This career transition allows me to combine my experience in education and digital initiatives with hands-on technical skills, positioning me to lead in the evolving landscape of technology-driven projects.
